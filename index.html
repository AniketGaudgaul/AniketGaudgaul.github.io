Research & Publications

📄 Published · ECIR 2024

MedSumm: A Multimodal Approach to Summarizing Code-Mixed Hindi-English Clinical Queries

A. Ghosh, A. Acharya, P. Jha, A. Gaudgaul, R. Majumdar, S. Saha, A. Chadha, R. Jain, S. Sinha, S. Agarwal

European Conference on Information Retrieval (ECIR) 2024 · arXiv:2401.01596

Introduces multimodal medical question summarization for code-mixed Hindi-English queries in a low-resource setting. We present the MMCQS dataset — pairing code-mixed clinical queries with visual aids — and MedSumm, a framework leveraging LLMs and VLMs to generate medically detailed summaries. Integrating visual cues yields richer patient representations and measurably better summaries, improving healthcare decision-making.
